    1. West Edmonton Mall

    - West Edmonton Mall (WEM) is alike the most well known attraction in Edmonton. West Edmonton Mall shopping mall located in Edmonton, Alberta. It is the largest mall in North America and it is the second most visited mall in Canada and encompasses 5.3 million square feet. West Edmonton Mall is home to more than 800 stores and services including twelve world-class attractions, two hotels, over 100 dining venues, the widest variety of one-of-a-kind retailers, and entertainment for all ages. It holds over 800 stores and services including nine world-class attractions, two hotels and over 100 dining venues in the complex,the widest variety of one-of-a-kind retailers, entertainment for all ages, and parking for more than 20,000 vehicles. The mall receives about 32 million visitors per year; it attracts between 90,000 and 200,000 shoppers daily, depending on the day and season. WEM most famous attractions is the Galaxyland, and th World Waterpark. Its worth a visit, even if you're not looking to shop. It's worth spending an hour to walk around the mall, checking out the Santa Maria, Ice Palace and Waterpark. Definitely check out one of the restaurants on Bourbon street for lunch or dinner.
